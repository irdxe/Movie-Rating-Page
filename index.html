Movie Review: Interstellar

Interstellar follows former pilot Cooper, who joins a team of astronauts on a mission to find a new habitable planet as Earth faces environmental collapse. They travel through a wormhole near Saturn, encountering strange worlds where time behaves differently. Humanity’s survival depends on solving the mysteries of space and time. The film blends science, emotion, and sacrifice in a race to save the human race.
